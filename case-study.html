CASE STUDY

  • Home
  • Case study

A test setup was established at IITM campus using esmito's software solutions & management platform. In the test setup, 10 vehicles (Lohia, Goenka, Kinetic, Electrotherm), 30 batteries (Amaraja, Exide, CBEEV, and Exicom) and 1 bulk charger from Exicom were provided.

The vehicle ply within the campus as "Hail" services. Customers can book a vehicle through the provided app and take a free ride. The services are highly welcomed by school children, students and other users of campus.


Some 3W in parking lot


Passengers in 3W driven by drivers



Benefits/Impacts

1.Increased awareness about the potential benefits of EVs
2.Outreach to different customer segments with different lifestyle and requirements
3.Enhanced understanding of user requirements through data collected

The vehicles have run around 30000+ kms and batteries are used for 1000+cycles. This has given more than 300 million data points for analysis

which led to valuable learnings and immense confidence that battery swapping concept will be a successful model in India.
