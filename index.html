India’s First Indigenously developed Charging and Swapping Infrastructure Management Platform

Esmito offers a B2B SaaS platform that enables real-time monitoring, control, and management of EV Infrastructure. The platform spans charging station and battery swapping management, battery performance analytics, business operations management, user management, advanced billing, data analytics & insights, web and mobile apps and can effectively integrate with any third-party system.
Esmito is housed at IIT Madras Research Park. With the technology and expertise to manage, develop and grow EV Infrastructure, we offer robust software solutions that can optimize EV operations increasing the business profitability
