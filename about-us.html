About Us

Digitization of electric mobility

Esmito is a cloud-based platform that enables real-time network monitoring, control, and management of EV Infrastructure. The platform spans public charging and swapping management, EV operations management, driver management, advanced billing, data analytics, web and mobile apps and can effectively integrate with any third-party system. With the technology and expertise to help you manage, develop and grow your charging network, we offer robust software solutions that can be custom-made to reflect your brand and reinforce your market position.


Esmito is housed at IIT Madras Reseach Park, backed by IIT Madras. The objective is to provide One Stop Integrated solution for Public Charging Infrastructure and Swapping Infrastructure.
